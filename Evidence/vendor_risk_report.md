# Third-Party AI Vendor Risk Report
**Vendor:** Aria AI  
**Product:** Aria Marketing Assistant  
**Requested by:** Marketing Department  
**Date:** March 2026  
**Status:** Pending Compliance Review

---

## 1. Product Overview
Aria AI is a generative AI platform designed for marketing teams. It drafts email campaigns, social media content, and customer personas using company data as context.

## 2. Data Handling
- Aria AI requires access to the company's **CRM database** to personalise content.
- Customer names, email addresses, and purchase history are sent to Aria's servers for processing.
- Aria's privacy policy states data is retained for **up to 12 months** for model improvement.
- Data is stored on servers in **Singapore and Ireland**.

## 3. Security Certifications
- SOC 2 Type I certified (Type II audit pending)
- ISO 27001 certification: Not yet achieved
- GDPR compliance: Self-attested only, no third-party audit

## 4. Contract Terms
- Vendor reserves the right to use anonymised customer data for AI training.
- No data processing agreement (DPA) has been signed.
- Liability cap: $10,000 USD regardless of breach size.

## 5. Requested Access Level
- Read access to full CRM database
- Integration with company email server
- Ability to publish directly to company social media accounts

## 6. Open Questions
1. Has Legal reviewed the data retention clause?
2. Does this tool comply with our clients' data residency requirements?
3. Who owns content generated by the tool?
4. What happens to our data if Aria AI is acquired or shuts down?
