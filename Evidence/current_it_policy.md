# IT Acceptable Use Policy
**Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** March 2019  
**Owner:** IT Department

---

## 1. Purpose
This policy establishes guidelines for the acceptable use of company technology resources, including computers, networks, email, and internet access.

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third-party vendors who access company systems.

## 3. Acceptable Use
- Employees may use company systems for business purposes only.
- Personal use of company equipment is permitted in moderation.
- Employees must not access, store, or transmit illegal content.

## 4. Email and Internet
- Company email must not be used for personal business or mass mailings.
- Employees must not download software without IT approval.
- Streaming services and social media are permitted during lunch breaks only.

## 5. Data Handling
- Confidential client data must not be shared outside the organisation without written approval.
- All data must be stored on approved company servers or cloud platforms.
- Approved cloud platforms: Microsoft SharePoint, OneDrive.

## 6. Software and Tools
- Only IT-approved software may be installed on company devices.
- Approved software list is maintained by the IT department.
- Requests for new software must be submitted via the IT helpdesk.

## 7. Violations
Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.

---

*This policy has not been reviewed since its original publication in 2019.*
*No provisions exist for artificial intelligence, machine learning, or generative AI tools.*
