# AI Governance Project — Leapr

## Your Mission
You are the newly appointed AI Governance Lead at FinSecure, a 500-person fintech company. 
The CTO has asked you to build an AI governance framework before they deploy a new LLM-powered 
loan decision system in 60 days.

## What You Will Build
A complete AI governance framework across 4 checkpoints.

---

## Checkpoint 1 — AI Risk Assessment
**Task:** Identify and document the top 5 risks of deploying an LLM in loan decisions.

Open the file `checkpoint-1-risk-assessment.md` and complete the risk matrix.

---

## Checkpoint 2 — Governance Policy
**Task:** Write a one-page AI usage policy for FinSecure.

Open the file `checkpoint-2-policy.md` and complete the policy template.

---

## Checkpoint 3 — Human Oversight Protocol
**Task:** Design a human-in-the-loop process for the loan decision system.

Open the file `checkpoint-3-oversight.md` and complete the protocol.

---

## Checkpoint 4 — Audit Trail Design
**Task:** Define what needs to be logged for compliance and accountability.

Open the file `checkpoint-4-audit.md` and complete the audit framework.

---

## How to Submit
When you have completed all 4 checkpoints, run the submit script:
```
bash submit.sh
```
