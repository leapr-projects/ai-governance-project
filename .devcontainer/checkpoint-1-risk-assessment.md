# Checkpoint 1 — AI Risk Assessment

## Scenario
FinSecure is deploying an LLM to automatically approve or reject loan applications. 
Before launch, you must identify the key risks.

## Your Task

- Give it a name
- Describe what could go wrong
- Rate the likelihood (Low/Medium/High)
- Rate the impact (Low/Medium/High)
